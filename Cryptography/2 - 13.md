# Descripción
>Cryptography can be easy, do you know what ROT13 is?
>cvpbPGS{abg_gbb_onq_bs_n_ceboyrz}

---
# Solución
ROT 13 se refiere al cifrado césar, el cual se basa en mover las letras para una equivalencia de las mismas, algo así:

| original | A   | B   | C   | D   | E   | F   | G   | H   | I   |
| -------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ROT 2    | C   | D   | E   | F   | G   | H   | I   | J   | K   |
Entonces, para el ROT 13 buscaríamos que esté desplazado por 13 letras, algo así:

| original | A   | B   | C   | D   | E   | F   | G   | H   | I   |
| -------- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ROT 13   | N   | O   | P   | Q   | R   | S   | T   | U   | V   |
